TheraCare: A Progressive Web Application for Mental Health Support and Emotional Well-being with Data Analysis Tracking

Capstone Project

TheraCare is a web-based mental health support platform created to help students manage their emotional well-being. It allows users to write personal journals, access educational resources, and engage in guided activities. The system also supports appointment scheduling with consultants for professional guidance. TheraCare is designed with a simple and user-friendly interface to ensure accessibility. Overall, it promotes consistent self-care and mental health awareness through technology.

TrackBack: Lost and Found Management System

TrackBack is a Lost and Found Management System designed to help users efficiently report, track, and recover lost items. It allows users to submit detailed lost or found item reports through an easy-to-use digital platform. The system matches lost items with found reports to speed up the recovery process. Administrators can manage and verify reports to ensure accuracy and reliability.


Civil Engineering Lab Room Reservation System

This project aims to develop an online scheduling system for the Adamson University Civil Engineering Laboratory to replace the existing paper-based process. The manual system was slow, error-prone, and caused scheduling conflicts. The online solution improves efficiency, reduces mistakes, and provides better access to laboratory availability information.
